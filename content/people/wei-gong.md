Dr. Wei Gong is an Associate Professor in Computer Science with expertise in machine learning and computer vision. He leads the Gong Lab in developing innovative AI solutions.

## Research Interests

- **Machine Learning**: Deep learning architectures, neural network optimization
- **Computer Vision**: Object detection, image segmentation, visual understanding
- **AI Systems**: Intelligent systems that can reason and interact with humans

- M.S. in Computer Science, MIT
- B.S. in Computer Science, University of California, Berkeley
