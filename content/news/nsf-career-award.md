The National Science Foundation has awarded Dr. Wei Gong the CAREER Award, which is one of the most prestigious awards for early-career faculty. This five-year grant will support his research on 'Novel Neural Network Architectures for Computer Vision Applications.'

## About the Award

The NSF CAREER Award is the National Science Foundation's most prestigious award in support of early-career faculty who have the potential to serve as academic role models in research and education and to lead advances in the mission of their department or organization.

## Research Impact

This award will enable Dr. Gong to:

- Develop novel neural network architectures for computer vision
- Train the next generation of AI researchers
- Establish long-term research collaborations
- Advance the state-of-the-art in computer vision

## Funding Details

- **Duration**: 5 years
- **Amount**: $500,000
- **Focus**: Computer vision and machine learning research

The award recognizes Dr. Gong's outstanding research contributions and his commitment to education and mentoring.
