We are excited to announce that our paper 'Advanced Neural Network Architectures for Computer Vision' has been accepted for presentation at the IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) 2024.

## About the Paper

This work introduces novel neural network architectures that significantly improve performance on computer vision tasks. Our approach combines the strengths of convolutional neural networks with attention mechanisms to achieve state-of-the-art performance on multiple benchmark datasets.

## Key Results

- 5.2% improvement in mAP on COCO object detection
- 3.8% improvement in IoU on Cityscapes segmentation
- 40% reduction in computational complexity

## Conference Details

- **Conference**: IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) 2024
- **Location**: Seattle, Washington
- **Date**: June 17-21, 2024
- **Presentation**: Oral presentation
