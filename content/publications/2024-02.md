This survey provides a comprehensive overview of recent advances in reinforcement learning for autonomous systems.

## Abstract

Reinforcement learning has emerged as a powerful paradigm for developing intelligent autonomous systems. This survey presents a comprehensive review of recent advances in RL for autonomous systems, covering theoretical foundations, algorithmic developments, and practical applications.

## Key Contributions

- Systematic review of RL algorithms for autonomous systems
- Analysis of safety and reliability considerations
- Survey of real-world applications and deployment challenges

## Coverage

Our survey covers:
- Deep reinforcement learning algorithms
- Multi-agent reinforcement learning
- Safe reinforcement learning
- Sample efficiency and exploration strategies
- Real-world deployment considerations
